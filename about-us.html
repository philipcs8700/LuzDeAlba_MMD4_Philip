Our story

Luz De Alba – a taste of Spain with a Danish touch

A lively Spanish bar in the heart of Málaga where tradition, flavor, and community come alive.

Founded by Danish entrepreneur Lars Soerensen, Luz De Alba brings the warmth of Spanish culture to every corner. From vibrant tapas to expertly crafted drinks, we create an atmosphere where friends gather, stories unfold, and every visit feels like a celebration of life. Passion, creativity, and care guide everything we do — in every dish, every drink, every moment.
